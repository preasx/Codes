How it works

Welcome to Game Hub! To get started, you'll need to select your username and a partner to compete against.

Once you've made your selections, they will be saved and you won't be able to change them.

Games Available

We have three exciting games for you to play:

  • Tic Tac Toe - Test your strategy against our AI
  • Rock Paper Scissors - Try your luck against the computer
  • Memory Game - Challenge your memory skills

Leaderboard

Compete against your selected partner and see who comes out on top! Your scores will be saved and compared to your partner's scores.
